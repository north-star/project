A guide for the open source community to navigate Windows

Welcome to the North Star Project

The North Star Project was started out of a DevOps Meet-up in Seattle. The goal is to provide a guide for two groups that sometimes have trouble meeting on common ground: The open source community, and the Windows community. We're hoping this can be a tool for both groups to work together and provide great products and services.

For this to be successful, contribution is a requirement. The Wiki should act as a starting point to get ideas going. Whether you have questions or ideas, please help us by collecting these in one area.

Visit the Wiki here to help contribute to the project: North Star Wiki
